  • Title: Interieur van de Portugese Synagoge te Amsterdam tijdens de inwijding, 1675
  • Description: View of the interior of the Portuguese Synagogue in Amsterdam during the inauguration on 2 August 1675. In the middle, on the east-facing teba, the chazan. To the right the torah scrolls, worn by men and interspersed by men with a torch or a candle, are carried in a procession to the holy ark (aron hakodoth or hechal). To the left of this a map of the temple, on the right a face on the outside. On both sides medallions with the names of regents of the synagogue, members of the construction committee and persons who performed ceremonies during the inauguration.
